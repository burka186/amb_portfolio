I'm Ava. I'm an artist, designer, writer, and creative technologist currently based in New York City, where I'm pursuing my MFA in Design + Technology at Parsons School of Design.

No matter the medium, I make work about what I care about. This can be anything from the cultural — toxic chemical pollution, feminism, and mass-incarceration, to name a few — down to the personal: finding friendships on Twitter and facing my irrational fear of deep swimming pools.

Special talents you won't find on my résumé include quoting the entirety of Hitchcock's North by Northwest from memory; raising each of my eyebrows independently of the other; and finding the best donut within a 10-mile radius of my current location.
