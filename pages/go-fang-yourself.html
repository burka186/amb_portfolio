GO FANG YOURSELF


Medium: Photoshop, InDesign


As an exercise in MFADT Major Studio I, we were given a random subject (in my case, Dracula) to research and design a small project around. What intrigued me most about the history and lore surrounding Dracula and other vampire myths was their persistence, which seemed to speak to a deeply held human need to imagine a future beyond death.


So, I created a speculative future of vampirism as a slick, anesthetized, and commodified modern process, imagining the ability to evade death with the click of a button as the logical conclusion of a progress-driven ‘American Dream’ ethos.
