JANET KRUPIN


Medium: Illustrator, Photoshop


One of my good friends, Janet Krupin, is an actress/singer/dancer on Broadway who is currently in the process of launching her solo music career. She asked me to collaborate with her on building her personal brand as she moves into performing her own original music.


Janet is an incredibly strong, charismatic, beautiful person, and I wanted her brand to convey that strength, with a hint of humor.
